About Us

Welcome to GHSS Kharah

HSS KHARAH was established in 1953 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in KHOUR block of JAMMU district of Jammu and Kashmir. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 12.

Empowering learners from grades 6 to 12 with exceptional education, fostering critical thinking, holistic growth, and a foundation for lifelong success.
